CALVIN "VIN" WILKINSON


Calvin “Vin” Wilkinson is focused on reclaiming the American Dream, restoring a sense of common purpose, and realizing that no obstacles can withstand the power of millions of voices pursuing their dreams. Calvin studied business at The College of William & Mary where he developed and taught new and innovative ways to optimize home lending. Since then, Calvin Wilkinson’s efficient, fast, and flexible home lending process has been responsible for providing over $1 billion in loans to those pursuing their dreams.
Calvin Wilkinson started working in the finance industry just over five years ago.As a young man out of school, Calvin was motivated by a single, simple, powerful, idea – that he might play a small part in building a better American Dream.
In pursuit of his mission, Calvin rebuilt neighborhoods destroyed by economic downturn and erected homes in cities famous for being among the most prestigious in the world. It was through these experiences that he saw all that is America converge – farmers and teachers, businessmen and laborers, doctors and lawyers, all of them with a story to tell, all of them seeking a seat at the table, all of them seeking the American Dream. After a time, he began to understand that our cherished desire to live the American Dream depended on the active participation of awakened professionals. It was with these ideologies and principles in mind that he ascended as a leading voice in the finance industry.
Join Calvin to take up the unfinished business of perfecting your American Dream. If you will join him in this quest and share his vision that the time is now to shake off our slumber and fear, then we will build a better American Dream for ourselves and future generations. Calvin is ready to take up the cause, and march with you. Together, starting today, let us finish the work that needs to be done, and usher in a new birth of freedom in your life.
Calvin is a licensed loan officer in the states of Georgia, Alabama, Florida, Colorado, and Texas.

QUENTIN JEFFERIES


Quentin Jefferies is a Loan Officer dedicated to helping prospective homebuyers achieve their lifelong dream of becoming a homeowner. After receiving his Bachelor of Arts degree from the College of William and Mary, Quentin was inspired by a simple vision; putting the success of others before himself.
His background in finance and public service has led to a unique path that has given him a fresh perspective on the industry. He began his career working for the Governor of Virginia to develop Virginia’s $107 billion budget and later worked as an apprentice under the Virginia State Treasurer. His selfless service at the state level led him to the Virginia Retirement System pension fund where he performed risk analysis and portfolio optimization techniques on a $70 billion state pension fund for teachers, firefighters, police officers, and state employees. After seeing the needs of the hardworking American people, Quentin wanted to give all people a voice no matter their past or present circumstances. Quentin prides himself on making the mortgage process quick and accessible to all. From people in the most challenging situations to new families hoping to move in, Quentin is passionate about building a platform for people to ahiechve financial freedom.
Bringing two distinct backgrounds together in a novel way, Quentin aspires to redefine how his clients achieve the best possible interest rate in particular market conditions. Quentin understands that buying a home is one of the most important decisions one will make for themselves and their family; and therefore believes it is imperative to connect with the people he is trying to help.
Quentin is a licensed loan officer in the states of Georgia and Alabama.
